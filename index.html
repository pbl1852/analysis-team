Data-Driven YouTube/Netflix Content Planning

StreamKUS Content Strategy Report

1. Introduction

This report analyzes viewer reactions to popular YouTube and Netflix content. Based on the analysis, our team proposes new content ideas for StreamKUS.

2. Data Collection

We collected comments from YouTube and online communities such as TheQoo, DC Inside, and FM Korea. These comments show how viewers reacted to different types of content.

3. Main Findings

Romance content was connected to emotional immersion and character chemistry. Thriller and crime content was connected to suspense and survival. Human and social dramas were connected to empathy and realism. Comedy content was connected to relatability and humor.

4. Evidence

The data showed repeated keywords related to realism, fun, empathy, suspense, and emotional immersion.

5. Content Proposal

Based on the findings, StreamKUS should develop content that clearly delivers emotional value to viewers, such as romance reality, survival thriller, human social drama, and short-form comedy.
